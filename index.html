About BimmerDash

BimmerDash is a powerful real-time vehicle data monitoring app designed specifically for automotive enthusiasts. By connecting to an external Low Energy Bluetooth (BLE) OBD-II adapter, it precisely reads vehicle ECU telemetry. With stunningly smooth, futuristic animations, it perfectly showcases core parameters like RPM, speed, and oil temperature right on your iPhone screen.

Key Features

  • Ultra-Low Latency Rendering: Optimized with SwiftUI and Core Bluetooth framework for instant, real-time data synchronization.
  • Racing-Inspired Dashboard: Merges cutting-edge and minimalist aesthetics to deliver an immersive visual driving experience.
  • Demo Mode: Test and showcase full data simulations with a single tap, even without hardware connected.
  • Premium VIP Subscription: Unlock a richer selection of customizable gauge styles and advanced vehicle sensor monitoring.
